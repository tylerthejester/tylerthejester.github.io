Hello from San Diego!

Made by Tam (Tyler) Tran

San Diego is a waterfront city with top-notch restaurants, beautiful ocean-sprayed vistas, neighborhoods that are steeped in history and culture, and a centrally located urban park. Whether you're spending a few days indulging in the city's myriad of beaches and surf spots, visiting the aquatic attractions SeaWorld San Diego and Mission Bay Aquatic Park, or exploring the inland offerings of the city, San Diego will not disappoint.

My Top 3 Places in San Diego (which are different from what listed above hehe):

  1. La Jolla Beach
  2. Coronado
  3. Seaport Village
